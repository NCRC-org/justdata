Financial Sector ? What We Track:
PAC contributions and stock trades involving financial sector firms over a rolling 12-month period.

PAC Contributions Include:
• Direct PAC-to-candidate contributions from financial firm PACs (e.g., "Wells Fargo PAC")
• Industry association PACs (e.g., "American Bankers Association PAC")

Industries Covered:
• Banking (WFC, JPM, BAC, C, GS, MS, USB, PNC...)
• Investment (BLK, SCHW, BX, KKR, APO...)
• Payments (V, MA, AXP, PYPL, SQ...)
• Insurance (MET, PRU, AIG, ALL, TRV...)
• Mortgage & Consumer Finance
• Crypto & Fintech Source: FEC Schedule A (receipts), STOCK Act disclosures

Financial Sector PAC $: Total contributions from financial sector PACs to this candidate's principal campaign committee over the past 12 months.

Does NOT include:
• Individual employee contributions
