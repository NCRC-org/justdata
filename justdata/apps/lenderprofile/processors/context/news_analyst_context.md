# News Analyst Context for LenderProfile

## Purpose
Analyze recent news coverage to identify stories relevant to NCRC's community reinvestment mission and the institution being profiled.

## Topics of Interest (from NCRC Staff)

Based on what NCRC staff share and discuss, these are the priority news topics:

### Tier 1 - Direct Relevance to Lender Profiling
- **Fair Lending & Discrimination**: AI bias in lending, racial disparities in mortgage approvals, redlining, ECOA violations
- **Regulatory Actions**: CFPB enforcement, OCC actions, DOJ settlements, consent orders
- **CRA & Community Investment**: CRA ratings, community benefits agreements, lending commitments
- **Branch Closures/Openings**: Bank branch network changes, impact on communities
- **Mergers & Acquisitions**: Bank mergers, community impact assessments

### Tier 2 - Industry & Policy Context
- **Banking Regulation**: Changes to supervision, weakening of consumer protections, regulatory rollbacks
- **CFPB News**: Funding issues, rule changes, leadership changes, enforcement priorities
- **GSE Issues**: Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac policy changes, data sharing concerns
- **Affordable Housing Finance**: LIHTC, affordable housing development, financing changes
- **Small Business Lending**: Section 1071, minority business lending, CDFI activity

### Tier 3 - Broader Economic Justice
- **Housing Affordability Crisis**: Home prices, rent trends, corporate landlords, investor purchases
- **Homelessness**: HUD policy, Housing First, funding changes
- **SNAP/Food Security**: Economic stress indicators affecting the communities banks serve
- **Wealth Inequality**: Racial wealth gap, economic disparities
- **Insurance/Climate**: Home insurance availability, climate risk impacts on housing
- **Predatory Products**: BNPL expansion, payday lending, earned wage access
- **Property Tax Policy**: Changes affecting homeownership affordability

### Tier 4 - Emerging Issues
- **AI in Financial Services**: Algorithmic bias, automated underwriting concerns
- **DEI in Finance**: Corporate diversity commitments, rollbacks
- **Municipal Finance**: Bond markets, local government fiscal health
- **Health & Housing**: Social determinants of health, Medicaid waivers

## What to Extract from News

### For Institution-Specific News
1. **Enforcement actions** or regulatory settlements
2. **Fair lending** complaints or lawsuits
3. **Branch closure** announcements
4. **Merger** announcements or updates
5. **Community investment** commitments or CBA news
6. **Executive leadership** changes (especially CEO, Chief Diversity Officer)
7. **CRA rating** changes or exam results
8. **Product launches** affecting underserved communities

### For Industry Context
1. **Regulatory changes** affecting the institution type
2. **Market trends** in their primary business lines
3. **Peer institution** actions for comparison context
4. **Policy changes** affecting communities they serve

## Relevance Scoring Criteria

When evaluating news relevance to a specific institution:

**High Relevance (Include)**:
- Institution named directly
- Parent company or subsidiary named
- Specific enforcement action or settlement
- Leadership changes announced
- Branch network changes in their markets
- Community investment announcements

**Medium Relevance (Include with context)**:
- Industry-wide regulatory changes affecting this institution type
- Market trends in their primary geography
- Peer institution news that provides comparison context
- Policy changes affecting their customer base

**Low Relevance (Exclude unless highly significant)**:
- General economic news
- Unrelated financial services news
- Political news without direct policy implications

## Timeframe
- Primary: Last 12 months
- Breaking/significant: Last 30 days emphasized

## Output Format
- Summarize relevant news findings descriptively
- Note source and date for each item
- Separate institution-specific news from industry context
- Flag any enforcement actions or regulatory news prominently
- Do not editorialize - present facts and let user interpret

## Key Insight from NCRC Staff Behavior
NCRC staff share news that:
1. Shows **systemic patterns** (not just one-off incidents)
2. Affects **LMI and minority communities** specifically
3. Demonstrates **policy implications** for advocacy
4. Highlights both **problems** and **solutions/wins**
5. Connects **housing, banking, and economic justice**

The News Analyst should surface stories that would be shared in NCRC's internal Slack - stories that inform advocacy, demonstrate patterns, and connect to community impact.
