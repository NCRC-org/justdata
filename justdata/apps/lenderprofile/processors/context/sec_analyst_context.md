# SEC Analyst Context for LenderProfile

## Purpose
Analyze SEC filings (10-K, DEF 14A, 8-K) for the ultimate parent entity to extract information relevant to NCRC's community reinvestment mission.

## Executive Leadership Focus
- **Primary**: CEO
- **Secondary**: Chief Diversity Officer, Community Development Officer, Community Engagement Officer
- **Note turnover** but do not interpret/comment on it
- **Note compensation** figures only (do not compare to community investment)

## Filing Types to Analyze
- **10-K**: Business description, risk factors, legal proceedings
- **DEF 14A (Proxy)**: Executive compensation, board diversity, shareholder proposals
- **8-K**: Material events, enforcement actions

## Timeframe
- 1 year of filings

## Community Benefits Agreements (CBA) Context

Since 2016, NCRC has facilitated CBAs worth $338 billion with 15 banking groups. CBAs are negotiated commitments that include:

### Typical CBA Components
- **Community lending goals**: $ amounts for LMI/minority lending
- **Affordable housing commitments**: Financing for affordable housing projects
- **Mortgage origination targets**: Specific goals for underserved/LMI borrowers
- **Down payment assistance funds**: Direct support for LMI homebuyers
- **Small business lending**: Goals for minority-owned businesses
- **Branch commitments**: Maintaining presence in LMI communities
- **Philanthropic pledges**: Grants to community organizations

### Example CBA Commitments
- NY Community Bancorp: $28B over 5 years ($22B community lending, $6B mortgages to underserved)
- U.S. Bancorp: $100B over 5 years (20% increase in LMI mortgage lending)
- First Financial Bank: $2.4B over 5 years (achieved 192% of prior CBA goal)
- TD Bank: $50B commitment
- PNC Bank: $88B over 4 years

### What to Extract from SEC Filings
- Any stated $ commitments to community investment
- Affordable housing lending goals
- LMI/minority lending targets
- Down payment assistance programs
- References to CBA agreements or community plans
- Progress reports on community commitments

## Output Format
- Descriptive findings only (no scoring)
- Note findings and allow user to interpret
- Do not separate positives from risks - present together
- Extract specific $ amounts and commitments where stated
