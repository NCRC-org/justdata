## Key Pricing Rationale

### DataExplorer as Premium Feature
- **Higher operating costs** (real-time queries, large data pulls)
- **More complex** (too complex for most members)
- **Cost recovery** - Premium pricing ensures infrastructure costs are covered
- **Clear value differentiation** - Premium tool for power users

### Member Tier Value
- Included with $900/year membership
- Provides core value (AI-driven reports) at low marginal cost
- Reinforces membership value proposition
- Drives retention

### Member Plus Upsell
- Targets 5-10% of members who need advanced analysis
- Low enough price point for organizations finding value
- High enough to generate meaningful revenue
- Clear upgrade path from Member tier

### Institutional Pricing
- Reflects bank budgets ($5,000-25,000 range from market research)
- Base tier serves smaller community banks
- Premium tier targets larger regionals with sophisticated needs
- Custom pricing available for enterprise customers
