About JustData

Empowering communities through data-driven insights

Our Mission

JustData is a comprehensive data analysis platform developed by the National Community Reinvestment Coalition (NCRC) to provide powerful insights into banking, mortgage lending, small business lending, and community development. Our mission is to make complex financial data accessible and actionable for advocates, researchers, and community organizations working to promote fair and equitable access to financial services.

Our Vision

We envision a future where data transparency drives meaningful change in financial services. By combining cutting-edge AI analysis with comprehensive federal data sources, JustData empowers users to identify patterns, uncover disparities, and advocate for fair lending practices that benefit all communities.

What is JustData?

JustData is a unified platform that brings together multiple data analysis applications, each designed to address specific aspects of financial services and community development:

  • LendSight - Analyze mortgage lending patterns and identify disparities in home loan origination
  • BranchSeeker - Explore banking market intelligence and branch distribution
  • BizSight - Examine small business lending data and trends
  • BranchMapper - Visualize branch locations and market coverage
  • MergerMeter - Track and analyze bank mergers and acquisitions

About NCRC

The National Community Reinvestment Coalition (NCRC) is a nonprofit organization that promotes access to basic banking services, affordable housing, entrepreneurship, job creation, and vibrant communities for America's working families. JustData is one of NCRC's tools to advance this mission through data-driven advocacy and research.
